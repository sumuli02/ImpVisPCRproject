Analysing qPCR Results

Introduction

After you obtain the qPCR data in .csv form, you will need to analyse and interpret them to draw conclusions for your lab.

In the following two sections, you will go through some videos and interactive graphs to build your knowledge about PCR data analysis.

Learning objectives

This learning tool aims to deliver the followig learning outcomes:

Section 1

  • Understand the phases of the amplification plot
  • Identify suitable values for the fluorescence threshold
  • Understand the correlation between parameters and the Ct values

Section 2

  • Understand the double delta Ct method and the significance of the calculated value ($2^{-∆∆Ct}$)
  • Conduct simple analysis of the data in spreadsheets and interpret the results
