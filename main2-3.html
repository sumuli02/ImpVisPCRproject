Section 2: Calculations

2.3 Interpretation of Result

In the lab, you probably took technical repeats and biological repeats. Average of these repeats are used to calculate the ΔΔCt value. ΔΔCt value is also called the normalised Ct value because it tells us the relative Ct value of the target gene compared to the house-keeping(HK) genes.

First ΔCt is measure by comparing the target gene and HK gene in the same condition. ΔΔCt is the difference between ΔCt in target environment(with drug) and ΔCt in control environment(without drug).

ΔΔCt tells the average PCR cycle difference to reach the threshold fluorescence intensity. In each PCR cycle, the gene expression doubles. This means that gives the fold change in target gene relative to the house-keeping gene.
